Overview

Purpose

Do you love collecting paintings from local artists but you wish you could browse them online? Are you interested in leather arts and crafts? I'm creating an art portfolio website for my father-in-law, a local artist in Colorado. He does watercolor paintings, oil on canvas paintings, and beautiful leather crafts. You will see his artwork through this website and can even order some of his ready-made paintings and crafts online. Go visit our website and indulge yourself in great arts and crafts!

Audience

Whether you're on your desktop or your mobile phone, you can check Kim's artworks and leather art! We welcome those who are local art collectors, artists, and even those who love some paintings to design their homes. We also welcome those who are looking for leather arts and those who want their own customized leather product.

Content

Home

“The aim of art is not to represent the outward appearance of things, but their inward significance.” -- Aristotle

Have you ever experienced going to an art museum and felt like you have traveled the world after looking at the art collection? Do you like browsing art pieces and appreciate them online? Or do you love to collect paintings from local artists?

Welcome to Art by Kim's website. Embrace yourself as you see Kim's personal collections of paintings and leather arts. Kim loves traveling the world and capturing the beauty of the scenery by drawing and painting them on his sketchpad. He uses art mediums such as acrylic, oil, sketch, and watercolor. For him, it is his way of capturing the wonderful memory of his travels. He wants to show everyone his art collection online and share some of his paintings with art collectors! Kim also has a great talent for leather carving. He can craft different styles using leather with bags, treasure boxes, clothing, customized belts, album covers, and a lot more! You can personally message him for a customized leather product.

What are you waiting for? Come check Art by Kim's collection and indulge yourself with great arts and crafts!

Paintings

Art by Kim lets you explore the world through its art collection!

Our artist, Kim, is a traveler and explorer. He has captured the different cultures, scenery, and nature in his paintings and sketches. He started to love painting as a young boy who wants to keep the memory of his travels using his watercolor and sketchpad, instead of photography. He has traveled to different parts of the country where he had most of his art collections. Some of these places are Colorado, Chicago, Hawaii, Arizona, Florida, Utah, and many more. Everyone is free to browse his art collection and fall in love with the scenes. We are selling his art collection for a good price.

Here's how to buy a painting from Art by Kim:

1. Choose your favorite painting that you'd like to purchase.

2. Download it or screenshot it, and send it to our email provided on the website together with your name, address, and phone number.

3. Wait, relax, and check your email and inbox for a response from Art by Kim if the painting is still available and for steps on how to pay.

Each art painting comes with a good frame and part of your payment goes to a charity cause for animal shelters.

Leather Art

Do you want a personalized or customized leather product? Or are you currently thinking of a good unique gift for your special someone or for a loved one?

Art by Kim has a wide collection of leather crafts such as leather carvings, leather hats, belts, custom bridles, boxes, albums, bags, coasters, keychains, clothing, etc. Name it and he can make one for you! He has created a variety of leather products for wedding gifts, leather collections, home decorations, personal use, and so much more!

If you're interested in commissions with Art by Kim for a customized leather item, here's how:

1. Think of what item you want to customize. (example: belt)

2. Think of a design. (example: bears or flowers)

3. Please specify if you want to include a name or other details to be carved on the product.

4. Message Art by Kim with all the gathered details with your name, address, and phone number.

5. Wait for Art by Kim to respond and we will let you know about the mode of payment.

A part of the payment goes to a charity cause for animal shelters. Should you have other inquiries, please contact us at artbykim@example.com or give us a call at 801-123-4567.
