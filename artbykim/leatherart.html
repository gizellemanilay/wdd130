Leather Art

Do you want a personalized or customized leather product? Or are you currently thinking of a good unique gift for your special someone or for a loved one?

Art by Kim has a wide collection of leather crafts such as leather carvings, leather hats, belts, custom bridles, boxes, albums, bags, coasters, keychains, clothing, etc. Name it and he can make one for you! He has created a variety of leather products for wedding gifts, leather collections, home decorations, personal use, and so much more!

If you're interested in commissions with Art by Kim for a customized leather item, here's how:

1. Think of what item you want to customize. (example: belt)

2. Think of a design. (example: bears or flowers)

3. Please specify if you want to include a name or other details to be carved on the product.

4. Message Art by Kim with all the gathered details with your name, address, and phone number.

5. Wait for Art by Kim to respond and we will let you know about the mode of payment.

A part of the payment goes to a charity cause for animal shelters. Should you have other inquiries, please contact us at artbykim@example.com or give us a call at 801-123-4567.
