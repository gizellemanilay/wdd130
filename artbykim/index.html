Welcome to Art by Kim!

Have you ever experienced going to an art museum and felt like you have traveled the world after looking at the art collection? Do you like browsing art pieces and appreciate them online? Or do you love to collect paintings from local artists?

Welcome to Art by Kim's website. Embrace yourself as you see Kim's personal collections of paintings and leather arts. Kim loves traveling the world and capturing the beauty of the scenery by drawing and painting them on his sketchpad. He uses art mediums such as acrylic, oil, sketch, and watercolor. For him, it is his way of capturing the wonderful memory of his travels. He wants to show everyone his art collection online and share some of his paintings with art collectors!

Kim also has a great talent for leather carving. He can craft different styles using leather with bags, treasure boxes, clothing, customized belts, album covers, and a lot more! You can personally message him for a customized leather product.

What are you waiting for? Come check Art by Kim's collection and indulge yourself with great arts and crafts!
